How you can help

RMC is 100% volunteer-funded and can use your help!

We help 1000's of creatures each year and rely on the support of our gracious donors and volunteers. If you adopt, you make the life of one suffering creature 1000x better.

Adopt Now!

We are the only organization around that is dedicated in helping mythical creatures in finding safe and everlasting homes. It is the unfortunate truth that these creatures are being hunted and poached for their magical properties that their numbers are almost non-existant.

We at RMC are aiming to fix this.

Rescue of Mythical Creatures, or RMC, has been rescuing and housing mythical creatures since the 1500’s. We help protect and nourish all forms of mythical being such as dragons, phoenixes, griffins, unicorns, sirens, fairies and more!

By adopting one of these magnificent creatures, you not only get a new fantastical companion, you also protect them from poachers and other people who seek to hunt these creatures for their own gain.

Our magical item fund

Can’t adopt? No worries!

You can still support our organization. Our rescued creatures naturally produce and shed magical items, such as scales and feathers. By purchasing one of these items, all funds will go towards the care of the creatures and support anti-poaching organizations.
